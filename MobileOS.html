MOBILE OPERATING SYSTEMS

What are operating systems?

Operating systems are essentially what makes the device understand what to do with it's components

Why are operating systems necessary?

Without them, the system cannot work. it will just be stuck in the motherboard's BIOS,
or nothingness if the motherboard dosen't have one(which virtually never happens).

Examples of Mobile Operating Systems

Some examples of mobile operating systems are: Android, IOS, BlackBerry OS, and Windows Phone (OS).

What is the Difference Between Mobile Operating Systems and regular Operating systems?

Aside from the fact that Mobile Operating systems are specifically made to work with mobile devices (less hardware intensive, they may be limited in supported hardware.
Otherwise, Mobile Operating Systems and regular Operating systems have only small differences really. They can work on any platform (given enough ram, a decent proccessor, and enough storage.), and can basically run your device well enough.
and here is where this gets complicated. YOU have to choose your OS, and this is where the small differences matter. If you want to type a lot, maybe Mac OS, or windows may be a better choice than Android, or IOS. What if you want to use apps like Instagram or Snapchat? Android and IOS would be a better choice.
